# 33.3FM

## Music | Media | Community | Ownership

**33.3FM** is a creator strategy and consulting framework built to help artists, bands, producers, labels, nonprofits, festivals, venues, and creator communities bridge from Web2 into Web3 without abandoning the platforms they already use.

33.3FM helps music-driven communities build sustainable ecosystems through brand strategy, AI workflows, content systems, fan engagement, rights education, music distribution, digital ownership, and modern revenue models.

## Mission

33.3FM empowers creators and communities to transform music, media, and storytelling into long-term digital assets, recurring revenue, direct fan relationships, and stronger ownership infrastructure.

## What 33.3FM Does

33.3FM is designed as an evergreen consulting service and operating system for modern creators.

Core focus areas:

- Artist, group, band, producer, and label strategy
- Web2 to Web3 transition planning
- Community building and fan activation
- AI-assisted content production
- Agentic music operations workflows
- Securing masters and organizing rights documentation
- Streaming platform distribution readiness
- Social media audio distribution readiness
- Retail, hotel, restaurant, gym, and venue music placement strategy
- NFT album and digital collectible strategy
- Fan membership systems
- Music release planning
- Catalog monetization
- Brand partnerships and sponsorships
- Creator education and onboarding

## Music Rights and Masters Process

Before distribution or monetization, 33.3FM helps clients organize the master and rights layer.

### Master Readiness Checklist

- Identify who owns the master recording
- Confirm whether the artist, producer, label, studio, or collective controls the recording
- Gather written agreements
- Confirm featured artist permissions
- Confirm producer permissions
- Confirm sample clearance needs
- Confirm beat lease or exclusive beat ownership terms
- Confirm mix and master versions
- Store WAV, MP3, stems, artwork, lyrics, and metadata
- Assign ISRC and UPC codes through the selected distributor when needed

### Publishing Readiness Checklist

- Song title
- Songwriter names
- Producer names
- Publisher names
- Split percentages
- PRO affiliation
- IPI or CAE numbers where available
- Lyrics
- Explicit content flag
- Instrumental status
- Language
- Territory notes

## Local Development

To work with the frontend environment locally, create an `.env.local` file with the following variables:

```bash
VITE_BASE44_APP_ID=your_app_id
VITE_BASE44_APP_BASE_URL=your_backend_url
```

Example:

```bash
VITE_BASE44_APP_ID=cbef744a8545c389ef439ea6
VITE_BASE44_APP_BASE_URL=https://my-to-do-list-81bfaad7.base44.app
```

Then run:

```bash
npm run dev
```
